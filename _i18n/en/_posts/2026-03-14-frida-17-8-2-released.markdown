A tiny follow-up release with a couple of fixes and polish across Android and
the Node.js bindings:

- android: Detect setArgV0() in anonymous memory too.
- bindings: Re-add .withTimeout and .combine to Cancellable, which were
  accidentally dropped when moving to autogenerated bindings.
