Quick bug-fix release with a couple of fixes to keep things moving smoothly:

- android: Handle `android:process` when matching pending spawns. Activities
  declaring a custom process end up with a zygote-forked process named like
  `com.example.app:foo`, while spawn requests are keyed by the package name.
  We now strip the colon suffix before matching, avoiding spawn timeouts.
  Thanks to [@pakbaz-dev][] for tracking this down and fixing it.
- package-manager: Fix parsing of scoped package names like `@scope/name`, where
  we were treating `scope/name` as the version range and fetching the registry
  root, ultimately failing with a misleading `missing dist-tags` error.


[@pakbaz-dev]: https://github.com/pakbaz-dev
