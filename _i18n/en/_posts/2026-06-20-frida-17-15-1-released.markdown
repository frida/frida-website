Quick bug-fix release with the following fixes:

- darwin: Fix an unused variable warning on non-arm64e builds.
- linux: Fix musl RTLD call-site discovery when multiple GumModuleRegistry
  instances are involved. We now scan the linker's on-disk image instead of
  live memory, preserving the original instructions and allowing chained
  interceptors to hook the same loaded addresses.
