Quick release with a couple of handy improvements:

- gumjs: Add Checksum copy and peek methods. Thanks [@mrmacete][]!
- defs: Add support for Capstone 6. Thanks [@kripticni][]!
- darwin-symbolicator: Add missing locking, and initialize module before
  jumping to cleanup. Thanks [@comex][]!
- qml: Suppress Qt `__yield` error on recent clang.
- barebone, compiler: Bump @types/frida-gum to 19.8.0.
- Bump FridaCore to 17.15.3.
