Another quick bug-fix release, addressing two issues in the Python bindings:

- bindgen: Fixed marshalling of option keyword arguments. None values are now
  omitted instead of being passed to typed setters.
- bindgen: Fixed collection-valued keyword arguments by mapping them to the
  appropriate select_ or add_ methods while traversing the parent chain.
  This affects specs, omits, externals, PIDs, and identifiers.
